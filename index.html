Mercator Studio for Google Meet

A project by Xing

Chrome Web Store Firefox Addons Greasyfork Scripts Mercator Studio
Mercator Studio gives you fine control over your appearance on Google Meet. Write text & emoji in front of your face: · Auto-adjusts size to fit any length of text onto the screen. · Auto-converts \sqrt to √, \times to ×, \cdot to ·, \pm to ±, ^number to ¹², and _number to ₄₂. Precisely adjust lighting and colors: · Exposure & Contrast · Temperature & Tint · Hue & Saturation · Sepia & Blur · Fog & Vignette Move the focus to where you want it: · Rotate & Scale · Horizontal & Vertical Translate · Pillarbox & Letterbox Crop Somewhat nice presets: · Concorde · Mono · Stucco · Matcha · Deepfry Scroll or drag the sliders to adjust, and right click on them to reset.
